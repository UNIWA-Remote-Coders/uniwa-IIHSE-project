Screen<br>
The Lenovo Tab P12 Pro features a 12.6-inch AMOLED Dolby Vision touchscreen display, narrow Bezels on all sides, 2K resolution, and a refresh rate of 120 Hz. Additionally, with a brightness of up to 600nits, you can use the device even in outdoor spaces.<br>

Sound<br>
With the four built-in JBL speakers and the help of Dolby Atmos technology, you feel the sound surrounding you.<br>

Gaming without limits<br>
The combination of a 120Hz refresh rate with the speed provided by WiFi 6 and the Snapdragon 870 processor, gives you the ability to enjoy games with demanding requirements.
