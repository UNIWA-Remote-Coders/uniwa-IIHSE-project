Smooth content tracking<br>
The screen of the Motorola Moto G23 is IPS LCD at 6.5", with a resolution of 720 x 1600 pixels. Based on these figures, you will be able to watch series, movies or even YouTube videos comfortably and without straining your eyes, while enjoying the viewing experience it offers.<br>

Immediate device response<br>
The processor of the Motorola Moto G23 is an octa-core processor and more specifically the Mediatek MT6769Z Helio G85 (12nm). This is a feature that will simplify your everyday life, as it will respond instantly to your demands and will not bother you with a lot of hang-ups and delays, while operating and using it.<br>

Downloads that you will remember for a long time<br>
The rear camera of the Motorola Moto G23 is distinguished at 50MP (wide), 5MP (ultrawide) and 2MP (macro). According to these, you take pictures of happy moments, but without losing the slightest detail in the background. As for the front, it's at 16MP, so you can capture carefree selfies, either alone or in the company of your loved ones.
