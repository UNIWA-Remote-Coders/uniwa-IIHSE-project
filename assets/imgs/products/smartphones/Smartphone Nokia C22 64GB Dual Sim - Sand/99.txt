The Nokia C22 is built with enhanced drop and liquid resistance protection. Capture the best moments of life with the rear camera and selfie cameras supported by powerful imaging algorithms.<br>

There is plenty of space for your photos, as the enhanced Android 13 (Go edition) comes with fewer preloaded apps. For the apps you want, the octa-core chipset and memory expansion capability contribute to maintaining smooth multitasking performance.<br>

Live more and charge less<br>
Don't plan your weekend around your battery - The Nokia C22 can last up to 3 days between charges. All thanks to the large battery capacity and the latest battery-saving features, such as app hibernation and HMD battery saving.<br>

Built to withstand the day<br>
The Nokia C22 boasts a modern aesthetic, but its design has more than meets the eye. Between the tough polycarbonate back and the hardened 2.5D glass on the front, there is a rigid internal metal frame. <br>Together, they provide better resistance to drops and an IP52 degree of protection against dust and leaks.<br>
