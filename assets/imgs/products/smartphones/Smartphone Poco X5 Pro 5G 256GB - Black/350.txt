Supporting a high refresh rate of 120Hz, adjustable brightness (1,920Hz PWM) for comfortable viewing and an expanded colour range (10-bit), the POCO X5 Pro 5G is the first model for the X series with a 6.67" DotDisplay FHD+ Flow AMOLED technology.<br>


108MP Camera<br>
Lightweight and slim to fit perfectly in the palm of your hand, it incorporates a 108MP main camera with a large 1/1.52" sensor and 9-in-1 pixel binning technology, which captures details at 108 million pixel resolution, providing ultimate sharpness even after multiple zooms. The imaging system is completed by an 8MP ultra-wide-angle camera with an 8MP sensor and a macro camera with a 2MP sensor, while there's a 16MP selfie camera on the front.<br>


Flow AMOLED Top Experience<br>
Dynamic, vibrant imagery for an exciting viewing experience. The 6.67" Flow AMOLED DotDisplay 6.67" DotDisplay with 10-bit support offers a smoother colour gradient and a more natural transition between hues for a stunning 1.07 billion colour effect. With a 120Hz refresh rate, high sampling rate and high frequency PWM dimming (1,920Hz) guarantees smoothness and relaxed viewing.<br>
