Combining Finnish design with high-quality materials such as sapphire crystal and titanium, the Suunto Vertical Titanium Solar offers proven durability and athlete-certified performance.<br>

Sustainable design from Finland<br>
Made in Finland, whose residents are accustomed to difficult and adverse weather and environmental conditions, the manufacturing process of Suunto uses 100% renewable energy sources. <br>

The best battery life<br>
The Suunto Vertical features a battery life that surpasses any competition: up to 85 hours of exercise tracking with the most accurate GPS setting.<br>

In daily mode, the Suunto Vertical accompanies you for up to 60 days of autonomy, which for the titanium models of the Solar series reaches even one year without charging!<br>
