iPad Pro features Apple's powerful M1 processor for breakthrough performance and all-day battery life. It features a stunning Liquid Retina XDR 11 inch display for viewing or editing photos and HDR video.<br>

Cellular models offer 5G speed support for network data connectivity.<br>

Meanwhile, the front camera with Center Stage feature recognizes you and keeps you in the frame automatically during your video calls.<br>

The fifth-generation iPad Pro 11 is primarily aimed at professionals and content creators, such as artists, photographers, designers, architects and engineers, who need a powerful and multifunctional tablet for their work. It is also aimed at demanding users looking for the best performance and user experience from a tablet.<br>

iPad Pro and M1, the perfect duo<br>
Thanks to the M1 chip, iPad Pro is a blazing fast device that uses advanced image signal processing technology and M1's unified memory architecture. With an octa-core processor and fast graphics, you can build complex AR models and play games with console-level graphics and high refresh rates.<br>

At the same time, it manages to be thin and lightweight so you can carry it everywhere with you without worry, thanks to its long-lasting battery.<br>
