Ultimate at-home entertainment tablet<br>

Intensify your media experience on the Lenovo Yoga Tab 13. Dive deep into different worlds with 2K resolution tuned by Dolby Vision™ and surround yourself with Dolby Atmos® cinema sound over four JBL speakers sweetened by Lenovo Premium Audio. Then play your heart out on the gaming-optimized Qualcomm Adreno 650 GPU.<br>

The Lenovo Yoga Tab 13 is aimed at users looking for a large-screen tablet for entertainment, work and fun. Its large 13-inch screen is suitable for video streaming, gaming and browsing the web.<br>

In addition, the built-in base on the back of the tablet offers a comfortable position for the tablet to rest on while watching movies and television shows but also using it for educational and work purposes.<br>
