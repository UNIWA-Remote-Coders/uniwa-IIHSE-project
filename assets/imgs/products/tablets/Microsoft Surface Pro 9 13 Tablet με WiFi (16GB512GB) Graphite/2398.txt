<br>

The Surface Pro 9 offers the flexibility and portability of a tablet combined with the performance and battery life of a laptop, all in one device.<br>

It features the stunning PixelSense Flow touchscreen (10-point multi-touch) with a 13-inch diagonal, 2,880 x 1,920 pixel resolution (267 PPI), a dynamic refresh rate of up to 120Hz, and Dolby Vision IQ support.<br>
<br>
In addition, it is equipped with LPDDR5 memory (RAM) from 8 GB to 32 GB, Wi-Fi 6E (802.11ax) connectivity, Bluetooth Wireless 5.1 technology, 2x USB-C ports with USB 4.0/ Thunderbolt 4, a 3.5mm headphone jack, and Surface Connect and Surface Type Cover ports.<br>

Finally, it features a main autofocus camera of 10 MP (Full HD and 4K), dual far-field Studio microphones, and 2W stereo speakers with Dolby Atmos.<br>

Unique Flexibility!<br>
The flexibility and portability are the main assets of the Surface Pro 9. Choose the viewing angle you prefer thanks to the adjustable built-in kickstand and watch movies and browse the web with the tips of your fingers. Then switch from tablet mode to laptop mode with a *Surface Pro Signature Keyboard (with Fingerprint Reader if you prefer for added security) and connect screens, charge devices, and transfer files through the USB 4.0/Thunderbolt 4 ports in desktop computer mode.<br>
